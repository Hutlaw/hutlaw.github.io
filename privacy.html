About This Site

This site is committed to transparency and protecting your privacy. I use caching to enhance your experience—allowing you to return exactly where you left off—and to improve overall performance.

Caching

Caching is employed to memorize certain data locally on your device. This helps the site load faster and preserves your progress and preferences between visits. No personal data is stored beyond this purpose.

Google Analytics

For traffic analysis, we use third‐party Google Analytics. This service tracks overall visitor data to help us improve the site. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored, and no data is shared with any other sources.

Open Source

My entire website is open source. You can view, contribute to, or fork the project on my GitHub repository.

Data Sharing

I do not share any information about you with external parties. All caching data is stored only on your device, ensuring that your personal experience remains private.
